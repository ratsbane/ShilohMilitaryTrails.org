Please read these notes carefully before submitting order form

  • Recommended minimum age for Shiloh Military Trails awards is 11; for hiking the treks, 7
  • The hiker must complete the ENTIRE trail on foot to qualify for an award. The hiker must also have completed the required reading and other specified requirements for awards ordered.
  • Completed credential forms must accompany orders for awards. Answer all questions on front and back sides. Both hiker and leader must sign credential cards.
  • The Credential form has all the requirements for each hike.
